THE PROBLEM

Buyers don't decide during the tour. They decide after — and that's where momentum leaks.

~8 homes toured before a buyer transacts

Each tour ends in unstructured, emotional impressions that fade within days — so the next step stalls. Post-tour isn't an event; it's a repeated decision cycle.

THE FUNNEL · A PRODUCT LENS
